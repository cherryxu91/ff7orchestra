Final Fantasy VII Remake:New York, NY

CONCERT POSTPONED

Though we had hoped to perform these concerts as originally scheduled, it is with an abundance of caution for our fans and performers that we must postponed these performances. We are working hard to reschedule the performance, and will announce the new date as soon as it is finalized.

If you have any questions, please email feedback@carnegiehall.org or call CarnegieCharge at 212-247-7800. Kindly note that email and phone volume may be high.

We all look forward to sharing live music together as soon as we are able to reconnect in person. Thank you for your patience and support!


-----

The FINAL FANTASY VII REMAKE Orchestra World Tour, presented by AWR Music Productions, is an electrifying new concert based entirely on the groundbreaking game from SQUARE ENIX. Experience all new symphonic arrangements based on composer Nobuo Uematsu’s beloved FINAL FANTASY VII music, and the FINAL FANTASY VII REMAKE theme song "Hollow."

This brand new music is performed by an orchestra and chorus of over 100 musicians, led by GRAMMY® Award-winning conductor Arnie Roth, with stunning, high-definition video scenes from the new game created exclusively for this concert by SQUARE ENIX.

Immerse yourself in the world of one of the most visionary games of all time with this sensational multimedia concert experience!
